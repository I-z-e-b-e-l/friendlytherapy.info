Built by the community, for the community.

Entries on The Therapist List are provided by members of the community, as well as providers who have submitted their information. Clients may anonymously rate a provider's competency in a given community on a 0 - 3 scale: 0 = completely unfamiliar 3 = super comfortable and familiar. Providers are not permitted to rate their own familiarity a given community using the rating scale, but may describe their level of competence in the comments section.

The moderator does not assume responsibility for community submitted content. To give feedback on this resource, or to submit updates, please contact us.
