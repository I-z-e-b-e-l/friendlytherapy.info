Boston-Area Mental Health Resources for the Queer, Trans, Polyamorous, Kink, and Sex Worker Communities

(aka the Queer/Trans/Poly/Kink/SW-friendly Therapist List)

friendlytherapy.info is a community project intended to empower Gender and Sexual Minorities to make better informed decisions about our mental health care, and mitigate some of the difficulties in accessing providers who are knowledgeable about our identities and communities.

The List exists because of people like you.

Entries are supplied by the community, including clients and providers. Your contributions are deeply appreciated.
