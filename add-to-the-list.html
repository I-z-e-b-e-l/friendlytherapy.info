Add a Mental Health Provider to The List

This form is intended to collect information anonymously from GSM therapy clients and empower us to make better-informed decisions about our mental health care. In order to collect information from folks who are the most qualified to speak to their experience recieveing mental health care as members of any given community, we ask you to self-identify if you are a member of a number of communities. We respect your privacy, and no personal information about you (or any information about the computer or phone you are using) is collected.

If your therapist is already on The List, please feel free to make an additional entry for them and add your comments and ratings.
