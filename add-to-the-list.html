Add a Mental Health Provider to The List

This form is intended to collect information anonymously from GSM therapy clients and empower us to make better-informed decisions about our mental health care.

In order to collect information from folks who are the most qualified to speak to their experience recieveing mental health care as members of any given community, we ask you to self-identify which communities you are a part of.

Your ratings are always anonymous. No identifying information about you (or the computer or phone you are using) is collected.

If your therapist is already on The List, please feel free to make an additional entry for them and add your comments and ratings.
